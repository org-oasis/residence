<p>The <strong>equipment provided in Skikda apartments</strong>, particularly in the professional furnished residences of Filfila, covers the full range of needs for a stay without dependence on a hotel or on-site purchases. Equipped kitchen, air conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, full bedding and free parking form the common base of Oasis Residence's seven units, with variations depending on the chosen format.</p>

<p>For a traveller comparing several listings before booking, knowing the precise equipment list avoids unpleasant surprises on arrival and helps choose the format best suited to the group: a couple, a family with a baby, or an extended stay requiring more self-sufficiency. This article details the full inventory by category, the differences between the seven available formats, and points to check before confirming a booking.</p>

<h2>The common equipment base across all apartments</h2>

<p>Whatever format is booked, from studio to F3 with terrace, an identical equipment base guarantees a consistent comfort level across all residence units.</p>

<h3>Equipped kitchen</h3>

<p>Every apartment has a fully equipped kitchen: hob, oven, refrigerator, coffee maker, dishware and standard utensils for the unit's capacity. This equipment allows preparing all meals of the day on site, an important budget advantage for a stay of a week or more.</p>

<h3>Reversible air conditioning</h3>

<p>Reversible air conditioning equips the bedroom and living room in all formats, essential during the hot months of July-August and useful in winter for heating too. Quiet operation is one of the criteria checked between each rental.</p>

<h3>High-speed Wi-Fi</h3>

<p>High-speed Wi-Fi is included at no extra cost across the seven units, sufficient for remote work, video streaming or calls with several connected devices at once. This is an increasingly sought-after criterion for travellers combining a holiday with a few hours of remote work.</p>

<h3>Bedding and linen</h3>

<p>A full set of bedding and bath towels is provided on arrival, renewed midway through stays of 7 nights or more. No personal linen is needed, apart from beach items.</p>

<h3>Bathroom and hot water</h3>

<p>Every unit has a private bathroom with continuous hot water, a point sometimes overlooked in certain coastal rentals but guaranteed across all residence apartments.</p>

<h3>Free, secure parking</h3>

<p>Free parking is available to all guests, a useful benefit for trips to and from the beaches of Filfila or excursions toward Stora and the surrounding area, at no extra cost and without searching for a spot.</p>

<h2>Additional equipment by format</h2>

<p>Beyond the common base, some formats include specific equipment that justifies a differentiated rate and meets distinct traveller profiles.</p>

<h3>The F2 Suite with jacuzzi and sauna</h3>

<p>The <a href="/en/apartments/f2-jacuzzi-3">F2 with private jacuzzi</a> adds a two-person jacuzzi and a Norwegian sauna, both for private use and included in the nightly rate, with no surcharge for the water or electricity these amenities consume.</p>

<h3>The F3 with private terrace</h3>

<p>The <a href="/en/apartments/f3-terrasse-7">F3 with private terrace</a> has an outdoor space exclusive to the unit, ideal for outdoor meals or evening relaxation, plus a kitchen sized for an extended family with more storage and worktop space.</p>

<h3>TV and charging outlets</h3>

<p>A TV is present in every unit, with USB outlets accessible near the bed in several formats, a practical detail for charging phones overnight without needing multiple adapters.</p>

<h2>Equipment for families and extended stays</h2>

<p>Travellers with families or on extended stays have specific needs that the standard base only partly covers, with a few points worth clarifying before booking.</p>

<h3>Baby equipment</h3>

<p>The equipped kitchen already allows preparing bottles and homemade baby food thanks to a hob, a refrigerator and clean water. Specific gear (travel cot, bottle warmer, high chair) is not provided as standard in every apartment: it's advisable to flag this need in advance to check availability.</p>

<h3>Washing machine and laundry</h3>

<p>Washing machine availability varies by booked format. For a stay of two weeks or more, it's advisable to confirm this specific point when booking; a laundry service may be offered as an alternative depending on the apartment.</p>

<h3>Storage space</h3>

<p>Larger formats, notably the F3 with terrace, offer more storage for extended stays or larger groups, a criterion not to overlook for a family travelling with several suitcases and beach gear.</p>

<h2>What the displayed rate covers</h2>

<p>All the equipment listed in this article is included in the nightly rate, with no hidden fees or surcharge for water, electricity or Wi-Fi consumption. No deposit is required on arrival, a notable exception in the Filfila rental market where other landlords sometimes ask for a security deposit. Direct booking is done via WhatsApp, with a one-night deposit paid by transfer and the balance payable on arrival in dinars or euros.</p>

<h2>How to verify equipment before booking</h2>

<p>A few simple checks help confirm that the advertised equipment matches the actual unit, whichever landlord is chosen along the Skikda coast.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Ask for the precise kitchen equipment list if a specific need exists (blender, steamer, microwave)</li>
  <li>Confirm washing machine availability for stays of two weeks or more</li>
  <li>Check the advertised Wi-Fi speed if remote work is planned during the stay</li>
  <li>Flag any baby need (travel cot, high chair) before booking, not on arrival</li>
  <li>Compare premium equipment (jacuzzi, terrace) between formats to choose the one that truly matches the stay's profile</li>

</ul>
