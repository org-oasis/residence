<p>A well-balanced <strong>3-day itinerary in Skikda</strong> combines beaches, coastal heritage and a wider excursion into the surrounding wilaya. The most efficient formula devotes the first day to Filfila and its beaches, the second day to Stora and a boat trip, and the third day to an excursion toward Collo or the wilaya's main tourist sites. This split limits daily travel while leaving real beach time each day, essential for a successful seaside stay.</p>

<p>For French-speaking travellers, diaspora members or anyone discovering the wilaya of Skikda for the first time, three days is enough to capture the essentials of the region without leaving frustrated at having missed the highlights. This article details a realistic day-by-day program, alternatives depending on travel profile (family, couple, friends), the budget to plan, and adjustments possible depending on weather or season.</p>

<h2>Day 1: Filfila, beaches and settling in</h2>

<p>The first day mainly serves to settle in and enjoy the beaches closest to the accommodation, without major travel. Filfila combines several assets that make it a natural starting base for the following two days.</p>

<h3>Morning: settling in and first beach</h3>

<p>After arrival and check-in, head to the nearest beach for a first swim. Filfila beach and Larbi Ben M'hidi beach (formerly Jeanne d'Arc), about 7 km away, remain the most accessible options for a relaxed, unhurried morning.</p>

<h3>Afternoon: Marina d'Or Water Park or extended beach time</h3>

<p>The afternoon works well for a visit to Marina d'Or Water Park for families with children, or simply extending the beach day for couples and travellers without children. The Filfila area, between the Rusica Park 4★ and Royal Tulip 5★ complexes, offers several dining options nearby for lunch or dinner.</p>

<h3>Evening: rest at the residence</h3>

<p>Ending the first day with a quiet evening at the apartment allows recovery from travel and preparation for the rest of the program. An equipped kitchen on site avoids relying systematically on restaurants, a useful edge to keep energy for the next two days.</p>

<h2>Day 2: Stora, port and boat excursion</h2>

<p>The second day heads toward Stora, Skikda's historic seaside resort, west of the city. The program alternates between discovering the port, a boat excursion and relaxing at the Grande Plage.</p>

<h3>Morning: boat excursion from the port of Stora</h3>

<p>The port of Stora concentrates most of the wilaya's pleasure-boating activity. A morning boat excursion, when the sea is generally calmest, allows you to follow the rocky coastline, discover isolated coves and sometimes sea caves only reachable by boat. Booking or negotiating the trip directly at the port remains the local norm.</p>

<h3>Afternoon: Grande Plage and Les Calatines</h3>

<p>The afternoon can be split between the Grande Plage in Stora, lively and well equipped, and Les Calatines beach, a complementary option for those seeking a slightly different setting. Both sites remain accessible by car from the heart of Stora within a few minutes.</p>

<h3>Evening: port atmosphere</h3>

<p>Stora in the evening keeps a lively seaside-resort atmosphere, with seafront dining options. It's a good chance to try fresh fish before heading back to the accommodation in Filfila, about a twenty-minute drive.</p>

<h2>Day 3: excursion toward Collo or the wilaya's sites</h2>

<p>The third day opens outward from Skikda, depending on available time and preference: Collo for its preserved coastline, or a closer heritage and sightseeing circuit for those who prefer shorter trips.</p>

<h3>Option A: full day in Collo</h3>

<p>Collo, west of the wilaya, offers a less built-up coastline and a different landscape from Filfila and Stora. A full day allows the round trip with enough time on site to enjoy it without rushing. This option requires a vehicle and suits travellers comfortable with several hours of driving in a day.</p>

<h3>Option B: tourist sites and heritage of Skikda</h3>

<p>For a shorter option, central Skikda and its surroundings concentrate several of the wilaya's must-see tourist sites. This alternative suits families with young children or travellers who want to keep free time for one last swim before departure.</p>

<h3>End of day: return and one last swim</h3>

<p>Whichever option you choose, planning a return to Filfila in the late afternoon allows one last swim before departure or the final evening of the stay, a good way to close the three days on a relaxed note rather than a long drive back.</p>

<h2>Adapting the itinerary to your travel profile</h2>

<p>This three-day program remains a flexible base. Several adjustments bring it closer to the group's actual expectations.</p>

<h3>With young children</h3>

<p>Favour Marina d'Or Water Park on day one, shorten the day-two boat excursion or replace it with a calm beach morning, and choose Option B (nearby sites) for day three. The <a href="/en/apartments/f3-terrasse-7">F3 with private terrace</a> offers the space needed for a comfortable family stay between two excursion days.</p>

<h3>As a couple or with friends</h3>

<p>The full program, with the boat excursion and the Collo option on day three, suits this profile particularly well, generally allowing more schedule flexibility and appetite for longer trips.</p>

<h3>Without a car</h3>

<p>Without a vehicle, concentrating the stay on Filfila and Stora, reachable by taxi or arranged with the landlord, remains realistic for the first two days. Day three can then become an extra beach day rather than a distant excursion.</p>

<h2>Indicative budget for 3 days in Skikda</h2>

<p>The main budget item remains accommodation, whose rate varies significantly by season and format, from studio to F3 with terrace. Cooking part of the meals in an equipped apartment noticeably reduces the dining budget compared with an all-restaurant approach. The day-two boat excursion remains the main activity expense to plan for, negotiated directly at the port of Stora.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Accommodation: variable by season and format, with a 10% discount from 7 nights if the stay extends beyond the 3 days</li>
  <li>Meals: noticeably reduced by cooking part of the meals on site, especially with fresh local fish</li>
  <li>Boat excursion: negotiated directly at the port, rate depending on duration and number of passengers</li>
  <li>Transport: fuel or car rental for day three, taxi possible for the first two days</li>
  <li>Marina d'Or Water Park: entry ticket to budget separately if the family option is chosen</li>
</ul>

<h2>When to do this 3-day circuit</h2>

<p>The swimming season in Skikda runs from June to November, with water around 26°C in August. July and August concentrate peak crowds, which can complicate last-minute booking of a boat excursion. June and September offer an interesting trade-off: still-pleasant sea, lighter crowds, greater availability for excursions and water activities.</p>
